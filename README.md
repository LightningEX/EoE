# Echoes of Ember
A dark-themed text RPG. Go on your own adventure with multiple endings. Create-your-own coming soon.

# How to Create Custom Items
To create custom items, open the developer panel by using Ctrl+Shift+D. Scroll down (if necessary) and click Custom Item Editor. Here, you can create your own custom items that you can add to your inventory, add to loot pools, or even export and share with friends. You can also categorize your custom items in Groups, available to swap between at the top. This is simply the first customizable option, with many more on the way.
